Our Mission

Most "product review" sites are garbage. They're written by people who've never touched the product, optimized for Google rankings instead of actual usefulness, and packed with affiliate links to whatever pays the highest commission.

HonestGadgets flips that model on its head. Every review on this site is built on real Amazon customer feedback — the actual experiences of people who bought and used the product for weeks or months. We don't do fluff, we don't skip the downsides, and we definitely don't write like ChatGPT.

How We Review Products

Our review methodology is different from what you'll find elsewhere:

1. We Start With Real Users

Before writing a single word, we analyze dozens of verified Amazon reviews — both positive and negative. We look for patterns: what do people consistently love? What breaks after 3 months? What does customer support actually respond to?

2. We Go Deep on Specs

We don't just copy-paste the manufacturer's spec sheet. We explain what each spec actually means in real-world use. "5500Pa suction" is meaningless until you know it picks up Golden Retriever hair from deep carpet.

3. We're Brutally Honest About Downsides

Most reviews bury the bad stuff in paragraph 47. Not us. Every HonestGadgets review has a dedicated "Downsides" section where we quote real complaints from real users — the stuff manufacturers hope you won't read.

4. We Compare, Don't Just Describe

Every review includes a comparison table against 2-3 competing products. We tell you not just what a product does, but whether it's actually the best choice for your specific situation.

  • Real user data. Every claim we make about a product is backed by verified Amazon customer reviews — not marketing materials.

  • Regular updates. Products change, firmware updates fix bugs, and new competitors emerge. We revisit reviews regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept free products for review?

No. We don't accept free products, sponsorships, or paid placements. Every product we review is selected independently based on what we believe will be most useful to our readers.

How do you make money?

Through Amazon affiliate commissions. When you click our links and buy something, Amazon pays us a small percentage — at no extra cost to you. This keeps the site running without ads, paywalls, or sponsored content.

Can I suggest a product for review?

We're always looking for interesting products. While we don't have a formal submission process yet, we regularly scan Amazon for highly-rated products with interesting user feedback patterns. If a product has passionate fans AND vocal critics, it's probably on our radar.
